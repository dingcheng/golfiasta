## FAQ

**How do I find a course?**
Search by name or tap the location button to find courses near you.

**Are GPS distances accurate?**
Distances are approximate, calculated from OpenStreetMap data. 
They should not replace official course markers or rangefinders.

**Where is my score data stored?**
Scores are saved on your device and sync via iCloud when available.

**How do I delete a saved round?**
Open Round History, swipe left on the round, and tap Delete.
